FAQs

Do pedestrians always have right of way?

No, but drivers must still exercise due care. Fault depends on specific crossing and signal facts.

Can I recover if I crossed outside a marked crosswalk?

Possibly, yes. Comparative negligence applies, and recovery may be reduced, not barred.

Why are pedestrian claims heavily contested?

Exposure can be high due to severe injury, so carriers aggressively dispute fault and causation.

What evidence is most important?

Video, witness accounts, impact-point evidence, and coherent trauma documentation.

How soon should I contact counsel?

Immediately, because surveillance windows are short and evidence can disappear.
