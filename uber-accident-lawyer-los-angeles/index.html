Liability and Legal Framework

Comparative Fault Still Applies

Even with commercial coverage, comparative negligence can reduce value significantly. Carriers may assign partial fault based on lane choice, speed, or evasive response. Clear reconstruction evidence is required to limit fault inflation.

Burden of Proof in Layered Insurance Disputes

A rideshare accident attorney la claim must prove negligence and damages while simultaneously proving which policy tier is active. This dual burden makes evidence organization critical.

Evidence Preservation: App and Trip Data

Rideshare cases require preservation of trip logs, timestamps, route data, and platform communications. Delay can create avoidable ambiguity about coverage phase and response duty.

Corporate Defense Tactics

Typical tactics include app-status uncertainty, policy-tier minimization, treatment necessity attacks, and delay under "ongoing investigation." We counter with synchronized liability and coverage documentation.

FAQs

Who pays after an Uber crash?

It depends on app phase and policy tier at the time of impact.

Does passenger status make the case easier?

Liability may be clearer, but insurers can still contest treatment and value.

Why is app status disputed?

Because coverage limits can change by phase and materially affect payout exposure.

What evidence should I preserve first?

Trip records, screenshots, police report details, and immediate medical documentation.

Can I settle without full records?

Possible, but often at reduced value and weaker leverage.
