# Form Setup – FormSubmit.co

The lead form uses **FormSubmit.co** — no signup, no account.

## One-time activation (required)

1. Submit a test form on the site.
2. Check `ial.leads.2024@gmail.com` (inbox and spam/promotions).
3. Click the **activation link** in FormSubmit's email.

Until you click that link, no leads will be delivered.
