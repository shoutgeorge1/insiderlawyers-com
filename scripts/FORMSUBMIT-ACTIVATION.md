# FormSubmit.co — You must activate before emails are delivered

FormSubmit does **not** send lead emails to `ial.leads.2024@gmail.com` until the address is **activated**.

## What to do

1. **Submit the form once** from the live site (home or PPC page).
2. **Check the inbox for `ial.leads.2024@gmail.com`** for an email from FormSubmit with subject like **"Activate your form"** or **"Confirm your email"**.
3. **Check Spam / Junk** — FormSubmit messages are often filtered there. Mark as "Not spam" if needed.
4. **Click the activation link** in that email. After that, FormSubmit will deliver all future submissions (and any stored from the last 30 days) to that inbox.
5. **Submit the form again** — you should receive the lead email. If not, check Spam again for the lead email.

## If you never get the activation email

- Confirm that `ial.leads.2024@gmail.com` can receive mail (e.g. send a test from another account).
- Wait a few minutes and submit the form again to trigger a new activation email.
- Contact FormSubmit: https://formsubmit.co/support

## After activation

Lead emails may still land in Spam. Add FormSubmit to safe senders or mark the first lead email as "Not spam" so future ones go to Inbox.
