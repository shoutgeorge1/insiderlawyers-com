Pedestrian Accident Lawyer Los Angeles

Pedestrian collisions are often catastrophic. We move quickly to preserve scene evidence, establish fault, and build full damages claims for long-term recovery.

"Pedestrian claims require immediate liability work. Delay benefits the defense." - Shawn S. Rokni

Where Pedestrian Cases Become Complex

  • Disputed right-of-way and signal timing issues
  • Visibility and speed reconstruction
  • Comparative fault allegations against the pedestrian
  • Severe trauma with long-term care exposure

Damages We Document

  • Emergency and surgical care costs
  • Future treatment, rehab, and assistive needs
  • Lost wages and reduced earning capacity
  • Pain, suffering, and life-impact damages
